A Happy Accident

Published on September 13, 2014 in rant

Last week, I was pleasantly surprised to find an email by a correspondent of Economic Times (a leading newspaper in India) asking for permission to quote a comment I made on Hacker News in the newspaper’s front page!

I hope they have India on their radar! It would be awesome for the Indian Startup scene to hear all those great speakers live!

Who knew that a comment on HN would end up on the front-page of a newspaper!
