A Mature Engineer

March 31, 2014

Brilliant article on what differentiates senior engineers. Every successful engineer I’ve met, upon finishing up a design or getting ready for a project, will continually ask their peers questions along the lines of: What could I be missing? How will this not work? Will you please shoot as many holes as possible into my thinking on this? Even if it’s technically sound, is it understandable enough for the rest of the organization to operate, troubleshoot, and extend it?
