Fun with SparkFun

Published on July 14, 2014 in side-projects node

This is about a quick hack that I did. Couple of days back, I came across a cool project called SparkFun on HN which is free online service to push your data. It allows you to create streams and then push data via a simple HTTP call. The primary motivation behind the service is to enable devices connected via Internet of Things to push data online easily. I had been crawling a few websites to need of latest remittance rates to my home country (the rates offered for remittance differ quite substantially from the current spot rates).
